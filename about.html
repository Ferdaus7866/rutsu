Meet our Founder

Sagi Ahiel, a dedicated guitarist, faced a common challenge for musicians: the strain of long hours on stage, especially while handling a heavy guitar, led to persistent foot pain. This discomfort stood in stark contrast to the joy of his performances and prompted Sagi to seek a solution.

His search for relief brought him to barefoot shoes, known for their comfort. However, he quickly realized that while they eased his foot pain, they lacked the style a performer like him needed on stage.As a musician, Sagi wanted shoes that were not only comfortable but also visually appealing.

As a footwear industry veteran, Sagi led Plaka, a women's sandal brand that sold over one million sandals since its launch, with the knowledge of how to create shoes with comfort and style in mind.

Rutsu, therefore, stands as Sagi's answer to a broader need: stylishand comfortable footwear that brings the benefits of barefoot design to everyone. The brand offers a diverse range of shoes that caterto anyone seeking the natural comfort of barefoot shoes without compromising on style.

The perfect barefoot shoes for everyday wear.
