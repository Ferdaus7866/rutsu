Rutsu Size Finder

Size Charts

Finding the perfect fit for your RUTSU shoes and sandals is essential for comfort and style. Follow these steps to accurately measure your foot and determine your ideal Rutsu size. This guide ensures you get the best fit, tailored specifically to our products.

You will need: A piece of paper, a pencil, and a ruler.

  1. Place the paper on the ground against a wall.
  2. Step onto the paper with your heel against the wall.
  3. Use a pen/pencil to mark the heel and the farthest point from the heel (beware, it may not always be the big toe).
  4. Measure the distance between these two points on feet with a ruler.
  5. According to the choice of most customers, we have reserved 3/8 inch of toe room between foot length and insole length.
  6. Find your shoe size per the above measurements by referring to the size chart for each product on our website.

*The size chart indicates the inner length and width of RUTSU shoes and sandals in inches.

Important note: Our size charts may not correlate with other brands. Hence we recommend following the above steps to determine your correct RUTSU shoe size.
